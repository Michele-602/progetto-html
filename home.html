What is Coltan?

Coltan is a mixture of columbite and tantalite, used in capacitors in electronic devices.

Coltan in Congo

Congo has one of the world's largest reserves. Its extraction is often linked to armed conflicts.

Environmental and social impact

Artisanal mining causes deforestation, child labor, and violence.

Solutions and alternatives

There are initiatives for the traceability and recycling of technological materials.
